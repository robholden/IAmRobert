About me

If you didn't guess, my name is Robert. I'm a full-stack developer from Reading. I'm 24 years old, and have a bachelors degree in Forensic Computing, but my time at uni ultimately led me into web development and my current job at Liquid Modules.

I have been a part of many projects that range from .NET web forms to Ionic 2 mobile apps. I'm a very versatile programmer with a very positive attitude. When I'm involved in projects I tend to bring structure and organisation (where applicable). I focus heavily on requirements and preparation because programming a project in the dark will eventually lead to a crash.

I am a very ambitious, motivated and enthusiastic programmer. I am always looking learn and keep developing my skills. Whether it's learning a new framework, language or just finding some new tips - I'm very open minded and always ready for a challenge.

Things you can always expect with my programming is that it's well structured, tidy and documented. I'm a sucker for learning new things. Currently I'm building an application with an Angular 2 front-end with .NET Core 2.0 as my api framework (integrating with Entity Framework).
